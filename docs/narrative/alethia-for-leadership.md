# Aletheia for leadership

## What Aletheia is

A **structured system of record** for assertions (claims), supporting material (evidence), and **explicit human decisions** where your policy requires them. It is built for **auditability** and **clear accountability**, not for automated verdicts.

## What problem it addresses

Organizations need to show **what was claimed**, **what material was tied to those claims**, and **what decisions were made under which rules**—without pretending that software judged truth in the background.

## What Aletheia does not do

- It does **not** replace judges, committees, or policy owners with automated scoring.
- It does **not** rank “better” or “stronger” evidence for you.
- It does **not** use semantic AI to decide outcomes in the default product model.

## Investment framing

Value is in **reduced ambiguity** about process and record: fewer disputes about “what the system did,” because the system does less interpretive work. Where AI appears in the ecosystem, it must be bounded by ADR-level governance—not bolted on as silent authority.

## Governance

Mechanical checks in CI (schema lint, ADR index, guardrails) enforce that the contract cannot silently drift toward inference-heavy features without explicit review.
